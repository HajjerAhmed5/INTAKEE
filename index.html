1. Community Guidelines

Our Mission
INTAKEE exists to give every creator a fair, open space to share ideas, stories, art, and conversations. Freedom of speech is central to our platform — but that freedom comes with responsibility.

What’s Allowed
• You may post videos, clips, and podcasts that express your personal opinions, creativity, or experiences.
• Constructive debate and discussion are encouraged.
• Educational, artistic, and news content are welcome.

What’s Not Allowed
To keep INTAKEE safe for everyone, we prohibit:
• Nudity, pornography, or sexually explicit content.
• Violence, terrorism, or promotion of harm or self-harm.
• Harassment, stalking, hate speech, or targeted attacks.
• Posting private or identifying information about others without consent.
• Spam, scams, or deceptive monetization.
• Content that violates intellectual property (copyrighted material you do not own).

Enforcement
INTAKEE reviews content only when it’s reported by users.
We may remove or restrict content that violates our terms, but final decisions remain with INTAKEE.
Repeat or severe violations can result in suspension or permanent removal.

Creator Responsibility
Each creator is fully responsible for their own uploads, actions, and opinions expressed on the platform.
